Born From The Rolling Hills.Brewed In A Garage.(Okay, A Really Nice Garage.)

From the quiet workshops of Dürrenäsch, something honest emerges. Sir Bier is born from the belief that beer should be what it always was: real, local, and definitely not made in a factory the size of a football field.

Discover our story

Philosophy

We keep it real. only real ingredients.

No shortcuts. No nonsense.

In a world of industrial brewing, we choose patience. No artificial anything. Fewer, better ingredients handled with actual care. It's not about brewing faster, it's about brewing right—tossing out what doesn't belong, and letting tradition do what it's been nailing for centuries.

Beer born from a region that's been perfecting quality for centuries.

Our foundation. Our flex.

Our journey begins in the heart of Aargau, where Swiss standards aren't just met—they're exceeded. (Because, well, Switzerland.) Local barley, regional hops, water that's been filtered by Alpine geology for millennia. We source within biking distance of our brewery because quality shouldn't need a passport.

When tradition perfects something, we don't reinvent it, we respect it. Duh.

Quality isn't made. It's brewed.

Sir Bier exists not to scale up, but to stay stupidly small.

We let time, technique, and taste guide every batch. (Plus a bit of stubbornness.)

The can is our high-five to the garage.
